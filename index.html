Sales Conversation.

JEVA doesn't wait for instructions. It researches, calls, listens, adapts, and acts — before, during, and after every conversation.

Before dialing, JEVA researches the prospect — their company, recent news, tech stack, and pain points — and builds a personalised conversation strategy.

📞

Autonomous Initiation

JEVA dials automatically when triggered — new CRM lead, email opened, card form submitted — and opens with a personalised, curiosity-driven introduction.
